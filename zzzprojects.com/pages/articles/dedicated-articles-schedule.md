# Dedicated Content Schedule

Below is the schedule of planned dedicated content.

When you choose a date for your dedicated articles or videos, follow these simple rules:

* **Keep at least 5 days between articles or videos**:

  * No content (article or video) should be scheduled within **5 days before** your release date.
  * No content (article or video) should be scheduled within **5 days after** your release date.
  * Example: If your content is scheduled for **June 15**, the closest another release can be scheduled is **June 10** (before) or **June 20** (after).

* **Limit to one article/video every 2 months**:

  * This gives other content creators the chance to schedule their content too.
